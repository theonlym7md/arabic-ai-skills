# Brand Engine: Hospitality & Tourism (لغة الضيافة والسياحة)

## Context
Activated for tourism platforms, national carriers, and hospitality mega-projects (e.g., Visit Saudi, Riyadh Air, Almosafer, NEOM).

## Core Philosophy: "Luxury in Simplicity & Ownership"
The language must evoke passion, discovery, and warm Gulf hospitality. It is user-centric, focusing on experiences rather than transactions. 

## Tone of Voice Parameters
*   **Focus:** The User (You), Exploration, Comfort, Exclusivity.
*   **Emotion:** Passion, Wanderlust, Welcome, Premium.
*   **Verbs:** Sensory and kinetic (اكتشف، حلّق، عِش التجربة، تذوّق، استمتع).
*   **Style:** Inviting a friend, but a very VIP friend. "وجهتك القادمة", "اكتشف السعودية".

## Semantic Reframing (إعادة التأطير الدلالي)
| Avoid (Transactional) | Use (Experiential) | Psychological Impact |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| الموقع / المكان | وجهتك | Ownership. The place is waiting for *them*. |
| قديم / تاريخ | إرث / أصالة | Connects the past with pride and high value (Luxury Marketing). |
| جيد جداً / مميز | استثنائي | Raises the ceiling of expectations. |
| بحث عن رحلة | ابدأ مغامرتك | Shifts the focus from the search process to the excitement of travel. |

## Rules of Engagement
1. **The "One-Word" Luxury Rule:** Luxury does not need long sentences. Use single, powerful words for headlines (e.g., "اكتشف"، "تخيّل") surrounded by massive negative space.
2. **Intent-Based Navigation:** Menus should be based on user intent (e.g., "الوجهات", "التجارب", "خطط لرحلتك") rather than boring administrative geography.
3. **Conversational Empty States:** If a search fails, do not say "لا توجد رحلات". Say "عذراً، لم نجد رحلات في هذا التاريخ. ما رأيك بتغيير موعد السفر واستكشاف خيارات أخرى؟" (Turn a dead-end into a CTA).
