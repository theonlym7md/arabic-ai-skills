# Brand Engine: Authority & GovTech (لغة الإنجاز والتمكين)

## Context
This engine is activated when the project is related to government entities, official initiatives, or national mega-projects focused on empowerment (e.g., SDAIA, Misk, Vision 2030).

## Core Philosophy: "Empowerment & Reliability"
The tone is not about selling a product; it is about building a nation and empowering the youth. It must sound highly reliable, prestigious, and forward-looking without being convoluted.

## Tone of Voice Parameters
*   **Focus:** The Nation, The Future, Data, Youth.
*   **Emotion:** Pride, Empowerment, Ambition, Trust.
*   **Verbs:** Strategic and active (نُمكّن، نبتكر، نقود، نُبادر، نستشرف).
*   **Style:** Inspirational and directive. Example: "نصنع قادة الغد", "البيانات نفط المستقبل".

## Vocabulary Matrix
| Avoid (Weak/Passive) | Use (Strong/Empowering) | Rationale |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| نساعد المستخدمين | نُمكّن الكفاءات | "Empowerment" gives power to the user. "Help" implies they are weak. |
| تسجيل الدخول للموقع | انطلق نحو مستقبلك / ابدأ رحلتك | Transform mundane tasks into visionary steps. |
| تم قبول طلبك | نرحب بك في مجتمعنا | Humanize the bureaucratic process. |

## Rules of Engagement
1. **No Bureaucracy:** Never use "بناءً على التوجيهات" or "حيث أن" in UI elements.
2. **First-Person Plural (We):** Always speak as a collective unified entity ("نحن" نبتكر).
3. **Clarity over Courtesy:** In notifications, be direct. "تأكد تسجيلك" instead of "لقد تم إتمام عملية التسجيل الخاصة بك بنجاح".
