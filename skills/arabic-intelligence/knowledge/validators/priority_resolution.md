# `Stage 7 — Priority Resolution`

When design, tone, or structural rules conflict, resolve them using this exact hierarchy (Top priority wins):

**Level 1: Safety & Compliance (Absolute Priority)**
- Never compromise user security or mislead for the sake of friction reduction.

**Level 2: Brand Archetype & Tone**
- Brand > Humanization. (e.g., A GovTech portal must remain authoritative and polite; do not use overly informal slang just to sound "human").

**Level 3: Clarity & Friction Reduction**
- Clarity > Aesthetics. (e.g., A longer, clearer button text is better than a short, confusing, but aesthetically pleasing one).

**Level 4: Humanization**
- Natural Language > Literal Translation. (e.g., Avoid robotic phrases like "تم الإضافة بنجاح").
