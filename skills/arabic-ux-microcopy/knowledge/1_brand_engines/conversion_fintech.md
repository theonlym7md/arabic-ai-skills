# Brand Engine: Conversion & FinTech (لغة الإقناع وإزالة التردد)

## Context
Activated for E-commerce, SaaS, and FinTech platforms (e.g., Salla, Zid, Tamara, STC Pay, Lean).

## Core Philosophy: "Frictionless Trust"
The primary goal is Conversion. Every word must be engineered to remove the "Pain of Paying", reduce cognitive load, and build absolute trust.

## Tone of Voice Parameters (B2C vs B2B)
*   **B2C (Tamara, STC Pay):** Targets System 1 (Emotional/Fast). 
    *   *Language:* "بثواني", "سهالات", "ولا تشيل هم".
    *   *Focus:* Instant Gratification, avoiding pain.
*   **B2B (Lean, Zid):** Targets System 2 (Logical/Slow). 
    *   *Language:* "منظومة متكاملة", "بنية تحتية", "نمو مبيعاتك".
    *   *Focus:* ROI, Security, Efficiency, Scale.

## Semantic Reframing (إعادة التأطير الدلالي)
Never use words that trigger fear or guilt.
*   **Instead of "Debt" (دين):** Use "ادفع لاحقاً" (Pay Later). This gives the user the "Illusion of Control" and portrays them as financially smart, not broke.
*   **Instead of "Buy":** Use "تسوّق" (Shop) or "اكتشف" (Explore).

## Vocabulary Matrix
| Avoid (Friction/Fear) | Use (Trust/Ease) | Rationale |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| ادفع 1000 ريال | قسمها على 4 دفعات ميسرة (250 ريال) | Cognitive Chunking. System 1 sees the smaller number and lowers its defenses. |
| ادخل بيانات البطاقة | ابدأ مجاناً / بدون بطاقة ائتمانية | Zero-Risk Bias. Delay friction until they are invested (Sunk Cost Fallacy). |
| مزود الخدمة | شريك نجاحك | Shift from a transactional dynamic to a collaborative partnership. |

## Rules of Engagement
1. **Anticipatory Transparency:** Answer the user's hidden fears directly below the CTA (e.g., "بدون رسوم خفية").
2. **Action-Oriented CTAs:** "أنشئ متجرك الآن" instead of "تسجيل". Sell the value, not the effort.
3. **Blame Neutralization:** In errors, never say "أدخلت رقماً خاطئاً". Say "عفواً، تأكد من الرقم وحاول مجدداً". Be a partner, not a judge.
